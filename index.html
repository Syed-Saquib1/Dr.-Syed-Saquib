About Me

Dedicated to advancing sustainable environmental solutions

Recently completed Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Tjandra Setiadi at the Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB), Indonesia, I bring a strong foundation in wastewater treatment technologies and a deep commitment to advancing sustainable environmental solutions.

During my doctoral work, I successfully integrated Anaerobic MBRs with low-cost aerobic post-treatment systems such as Downflow Hanging Sponge (DHS) columns and Electrowetland reactors for the treatment of dye-laden textile wastewater. My work resulted in multiple high-impact publications and presentations at international forums.
